# R&D Career Report - Quick Reference Guide

## 📋 15-Section Report Structure

### 1. Career Overview
**What you get:** 4-5 sentences explaining the career, daily work, unique aspects, and who it's best for.

### 2. Industry Scope
**What you get:**
- Global demand with statistics
- India-specific opportunities
- 7 top companies
- Market size (₹ crores / $ billions)
- Growth rate (% per year)

### 3. Required Skills
**What you get:**
- 7 technical skills
- 5 soft skills
- 6 tools/technologies

### 4. Learning Roadmap
**What you get:**
- 4-5 phases
- Duration per phase
- Focus areas
- 4 actions per phase

### 5. Educational Path
**What you get:**
- Minimum degree required
- 3 preferred degrees
- 6-8 top colleges (with fees, rankings, location)
- 4-6 entrance exams (with pattern, prep time)
- 4-5 certifications (with cost, issuer, validity)

### 6. Research Methodology
**What you get:**
- How R&D works in this field
- 6-step research process
- 5 research tools
- 4 key journals
- Hot research areas

### 7. Career Paths
**What you get:**
- 5-7 specific job roles
- Salary for each level
- Companies hiring
- Required skills
- Day-to-day work

### 8. Salary Analysis
**What you get:**
- Entry Level: ₹X-Y LPA (0-2 years)
- Mid Level: ₹X-Y LPA (2-5 years)
- Senior Level: ₹X-Y LPA (5-10 years)
- Expert Level: ₹X-Y LPA (10+ years)
- Freelance rates
- Global salaries ($USD)

### 9. Industry vs Academic
**What you get:**
- Industry R&D: 4 pros, 3 cons, top companies
- Academic R&D: 4 pros, 3 cons, top institutions
- Honest recommendation

### 10. Challenges and Risks
**What you get:**
- 5 specific challenges
- Why they're difficult
- How to overcome them
- Timeline to solve

### 11. Opportunities and Trends
**What you get:**
- 5 current opportunities
- 5 future trends (2025-2030)
- 4 emerging roles
- 4 funding sources
- 3 market drivers

### 12. Practical Exposure
**What you get:**
- 4 internship platforms
- 4 portfolio projects
- 3 open-source areas
- 4 competitions
- 3 research opportunities

### 13. Case Studies
**What you get:**
- 3 detailed success stories
- Background, journey, outcome
- Timeline and lessons
- Real-world examples

### 14. Networking and Community
**What you get:**
- 4 communities/forums
- 4 conferences/events
- Online platforms (LinkedIn, Reddit, Discord)
- 4 mentorship tips
- 3 professional associations

### 15. Action Plan
**What you get:**
- Week 1: 4 immediate actions
- Month 1: Goal + 3 actions
- Month 3: Goal + 3 actions
- Month 6: Goal + 3 actions
- Year 1: Goal + 3 actions
- Final advice with encouragement
